about me


Hi! My name is Vivian Dai. I'm currently a senior at Cornell University, where I study computer science. My interests lie in computational sustainability: using software, mathematics, and data to make informed decisions about problems impacting the earth's future.

After graduation, I will be starting as a software engineer on Google Cloud AI . I'll also be moving to Seattle. Although I've never been there before, I am excited to experience all of the hikes, nature, and rainy days! I grew up in Toronto (which is still my favourite city to this day).

Apart from computer science, I'm also studying english (as a minor), and horticulture (for fun). I love creative writing, especially memoir. While I have not published any works, I love to talk about the writing of others. I hope that one day, I will write a story that I want to show the world.

I'm an avid hiker as well. Some of the beautiful places that I have had the privilege to hike are Bryce Canyon, Yosemite, Pinnacles, Sequoia, Shenandoah, Huang Shan, Hua Shan, and Northern Ontario (and many more!). The next place on my bucket list is Zhangjiajie.
I love gardening, hiking, and all things outdoors!
I am allergic to cats, pollen, and lobster.
I love reading and writing short stories.
I was born in China and immigrated to Canada when I was two years old.
